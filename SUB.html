Descriptive Statistics about Data Set

Healthcare as a percentage of GDP in the United States has increased from 5% in 1960 to 18.2% in 2018.

Variables in the dataset are: provider street address, provider city, provider state, provider zip code,
hospital referral region description, total discharges, averaged covered charges,
average total payments, average medicare payments.
